My Pet.

My pet, Lisa, is a cat. I'd like to think that despite her Australian origin, she is quickly getting used to life in Kenya.

Infact, I would say that she loves it here evident by how excited she gets when a chance to hunt the numerous rats in our cereal store emerges.

Her favotite toy is the rubber softball that she is always chasing around whenever she is not asleep

Also, she would never miss a chance to play fetch especially when its warm outside. This game particularly portrays not only her agility but also her quick reflexes. She is a true testament to the vast number of ways in which animals can be great friends with humans.
